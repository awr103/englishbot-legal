Privacy Policy

EnglishBot (the "App") collects and stores the following data when you authorise it via TikTok OAuth:

  • Your TikTok access_token & refresh_token
  • Your TikTok open_id
  • Aggregated video-level analytics (views, likes, comments)

This data is used solely to generate and upload content and to optimise future videos. It is stored in an encrypted database on AWS (Singapore) and is never shared with third parties.

You may request data deletion at any time by emailing riegeraw@gmail.com. Requests are processed within 7 days.
